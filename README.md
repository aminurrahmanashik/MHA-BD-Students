# MHA-BD-Students
### Suicidal Risk Survey Dataset: Bangladeshi University Students


Survey dataset and accompanying code for suicidal risk classification among university students in Bangladesh. Data were collected across higher education institutions (May–August 2025) and cover demographic, academic, family, and psychological dimensions. The survey instrument was reviewed by a practising psychiatrist prior to deployment.

The dataset contains **4,004 records**. The target variable distinguishes Risk Positive (suicidal ideation, planning, or attempt; 67.7%) from Risk Negative (no ideation; 32.3%).

Full methodological details, experimental results, and analysis are reported in the associated manuscript, currently under review. A reference will be added upon publication.

## Ethics

All participants provided voluntary written informed consent. Responses were fully anonymised; no personally identifiable information was recorded. A crisis helpline number was displayed throughout the survey. This study was conducted in accordance with the ethical principles of the Declaration of Helsinki.
